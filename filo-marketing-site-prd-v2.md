## 1. Context & Goal

Filo is an iOS meeting assistant that helps busy professionals turn every conversation into clear, actionable next steps ‚Äî fast, private, and mobile-first.

Core capabilities:

- Record meetings from your iPhone  
- Generate instant on-device AI summaries  
- Extract clear action items  
- Sync tasks to Apple Reminders (Free & Pro)  
- Draft follow-up emails (Pro only)  
- Use advanced ‚ÄúSales Mode‚Äù summarization (Pro only)

**Goal of this website**

Build a high-conversion marketing site that:

1. Explains what Filo does in under 5 seconds  
2. Drives clicks to the App Store (primary CTA)  
3. Clearly presents Free vs Pro features and value  
4. Builds trust around privacy (on-device AI, no cloud recordings)  
5. Provides a clear structure for docs, support, and future content
